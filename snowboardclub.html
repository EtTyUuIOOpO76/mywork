Board with Friends!

Snowboarding Club is about snowboarding and making friends. We're a unique type of club

in that we meet on the mountain and are shamelessly about having a good time on the

snow and making sure those around us have an awesome time too.

Snowboarding is Community!

The Snowboarding Club philosophy is about creating community. When snow season

arrives, it's often difficult to coordinate trips to the mountain with your friends and regular

snowboard buddies. Snowboarding Club means you'll always have "buddies" at the

mountain. And when you're at the mountain, you'll have someone else's back too.
